Micro Retreats™

A 2–3 hour guided journey that weaves together meditation, a gentle hike, forest-bathing techniques, and yoga — all the restoration of a weekend retreat, in an afternoon.

Check Availability

The experience

Slow down. Look up. Breathe.

Your guide leads a small group along one of Western North Carolina's most beautiful trails, pausing for grounding meditation, sensory forest-bathing practices, and a flowing yoga session in a setting you'll never forget.

No experience necessary — every Micro Retreat is tailored to the group, whether it's your first time on a mat or your hundredth.
